Import PDFs into your Zotero library.

If you already have a PDF of an article on your computer, you can add it to your collection by dragging it directly into Zotero.

You can even try to retrieve the key citation information directly from the PDF by right-clicking on it and choosing “Retreive Metadata for PDF.” Depending on how the PDF was created, Zotero may be able to collect this information for you!

Global State

Set data-state="something" on a slide and "something" will be added as a class to the document element when the slide is open. This lets you apply broader style changes, like switching the page background.

State Events

Additionally custom events can be triggered on a per slide basis by binding to the data-state name. 


                        Reveal.addEventListener( 'customevent', function() {
                        console.log( '"customevent" has fired' );
                        } );
